Sometimes choosing an anime to watch can be confusing.


Anime Dashboard makes it easier by showing you all the information you need, in order to choose something you want!


The information focuses on a few important factors to help. Popularity, average user rating, genre, type (movie, tv etc...) and number of episodes.


The data is static. If you would like to know more about the data it comes from Kaggle


Definitions of genre and terminology were taken from google, wikepidia and Reelrundown
