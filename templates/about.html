We understand that sometimes choosing an
anime to watch can be a difficult process,
exacerbated by wierd titles, foreign words and
a sense of “I don’t know what I’m in the mood for”.




Anime Dashboard stats can help you choose something
totally awsome to watch without having to ponder over
lengthy synopsises,confusing titles or even think too
much about what it is you are *truly* in the mood for.




ANIME DASHBOARD focuses on three important pieces
of data to help you out here. The first is popularity
(the number of people who have joined the group related
to the anime), the second is average user rating
(the average score out of 10 each user has rated the anime),
and the third is genre.




Anime dashboard got its data from
https://www.kaggle.com/CooperUnion/anime-recommendations-database
Which got it’s data from MyAnimeList.net




Definitions of genre and terminology were taken from
google and wikepidia.
